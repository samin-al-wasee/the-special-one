# ⚽ The Special One — High-Level Concept

---

## 📝 A. Non-Functional / Product Constraints

1. 📴 **Offline Game**  
   - Core gameplay must function fully offline.  

2. 📚 **Fictional, Real-World-Inspired, Editable Game DB**  
   - Football world is fictional but reflects real-world logic.  
   - Game DB should allow future edits to players, teams, competitions.  

---

## 🎮 B. Game Modes

1. 🏆 **Tournament Mode** — Lighter, competition-focused mode.  
2. 🏟️ **Club Mode** — Full long-term club management.  
3. 🇺🇳 **National Mode** — Full long-term national team management, focused only on national team responsibilities.  
4. 🔀 **Hybrid Mode** — Club + National management in a shared football world.  

---

## ⚙️ C. Core Gameplay Systems

1. ⚡ **Match Engine**  
   - Simulates matches using squads, tactics, and player attributes.  
   - Produces results: score, goals, assists, cards, injuries, key match events.  
   - Supports different match types: friendly, league, cup, international tournament.  
   - Optional: weather/pitch conditions, fixture congestion effects.  

2. 📋 **Squad Management and Tactics**  
   - Assign players to positions, define tactics and formations.  
   - Options: formations, player roles, team strategies, advanced instructions.  
   - Squad rotation, player morale, team cohesion, captain influence, dressing room conflicts.  
   - Substitutions, player fatigue, and rotation management.  

3. 📊 **Team & Player Statistics System (Text-Based)**  
   - Tracks detailed match, season, and career statistics.  
   - **Team Stats:** possession, shots, xG, pass accuracy, fouls, cards, corners, offsides, goals, injuries.  
   - **Player Stats:** goals, assists, passes, tackles, shots, xG contribution, minutes, cards, injuries, player rating.  
   - **Season/Career Aggregates:** historical stats for players, teams, and managers.  
   - Influences awards, morale, manager reputation, player progression.  
   - Fully text-based: match reports, newsfeed, post-match summaries, season reports.  

4. 🏥 **Injuries and Recovery System**  
   - Text-based injury system: type, severity, recovery time.  
   - Recurring injuries and fitness management.  

---

## 🏢 D. Club/National Management Systems (Where Applicable)

1. 💰 **Finance**  
   - Club finances: income, expenses, sponsorships, ticket sales.  
   - Sponsor/commercial deals (contracts, bonuses).  

2. 🏋️ **Structures and Facilities**  
   - Training, medical, and coaching facilities.  
   - Affects player performance, injury recovery, and progression.  
   - Optional: custom training plans per player or squad.  

3. 🔄 **Transfer Market**  
   - Player buying, selling, contract management.  
   - Optional: loan system, negotiations, player valuations.  

4. 🔍 **Scouting**  
   - Discover new players, assess talent, assign scouts.  
   - Optional: scouting youth and international players for future squads.  

5. 📱 **Social Media / Reputation System**  
   - Reflects club/national team reputation, news, and fan engagement.  
   - Fully text-based.  

6. 👥 **Fan/Board Objectives and Confidence/Satisfaction System**  
   - Tracks fan and board expectations.  
   - Measures satisfaction, confidence, and manager reputation.  

7. 📰 **Media/Fan Interaction and Press Conferences**  
   - Interaction with journalists and fans.  
   - Affects team morale, fan confidence, and reputation.  
   - Fully text-based (question-answer style).  

8. 👶 **Youth Development**  
   - Manage youth academy and player progression.  
   - Promote youth players to first team.  
   - Optional: youth scouting, development focus per training facility.  

9. 🏁 **Competition & League Management**  
   - Scheduling, league tables, relegation/promotion, cup draws.  
   - Applicable to Club, National, and Tournament modes.  
   - Includes international qualifiers and friendly match calendars.  

10. 📈 **Manager Career Progression**  
    - Tracks manager record, reputation, and offers from clubs/national teams.  

11. 🏅 **Achievements / Milestones / Challenges**  
    - Optional long-term goals (win X league, promote Y youth, etc.) to increase replayability.  

12. 📆 **Season & Calendar Management**  
    - Fixture congestion, rest days, player rotation, international breaks.  

13. 🌍 **Random Events / Dynamic World Events**  
    - Unexpected events: stadium upgrades, youth talents appearing, player scandals.  

14. 🏆 **Awards System (Text-Based)**  
    - Recognizes outstanding performance of players, teams, and managers.  
    - Types of awards:  
        - **Short-Term / Recurring:** Player of the Week, Team of the Week, Manager of the Week  
        - **Seasonal / Long-Term:** Player of the Season, Player of the Year, Manager of the Season, Team of the Season  
        - **Optional:** Best Young Player, Top Goal Scorer, Fair Play Award  
    - Scope: Per League, Per Continent, Global  
    - Text-based presentation via notifications, newsfeed, or season summaries.  
    - Affects morale, reputation, and club/national prestige.

- Advanced match analytics (expected assists, pass networks, shot maps in text)
